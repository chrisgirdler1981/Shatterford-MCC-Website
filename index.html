Rallies

Shatterford Motorcycle Club, rooted in its rich history of rallying, continues its tradition of adventure and camaraderie by actively participating in motorcycle rallies across the UK. From its humble beginnings organizing joint rally activities to its present-day presence at various national and regional rally events, the club remains steadfast in its commitment to the thrill of the ride and the bonds forged at these events.

Club Rides Out

Embarking on regular club rides is a cherished tradition at Shatterford Motorcycle Club. Our rides span from local scenic routes to adventurous cross-country journeys, offering members an array of experiences on two wheels. Recently, two of our members embarked on an exhilarating journey to Scotland astride classic and modern 125cc machines, showcasing the spirit of adventure and the enduring bond of camaraderie within our club.

Club Social meetings

At Shatterford Motorcycle Club, our traditions are as vibrant as our rides. We gather regularly for our cherished 'Club night,' a time-honored tradition where members come together to share stories, plan adventures, and strengthen friendships. In addition to our club nights, we attend various events and meetings, providing opportunities for camaraderie and collaboration. Whenever possible, we also join bike nights, extending our passion for motorcycles beyond our club and into the broader riding community
